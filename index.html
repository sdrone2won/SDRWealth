About SDR

I am not a guru. I do not have a camera crew. I am a student currently fighting to save my academic trajectory before the final deadline.

SDRWealth began as a documentation of high-performance, but in late 2024, I hit a wall. Dopamine burnout. The gap between my potential and my output became unbearable.

I went dark to code these weapons (AFM) not to sell them, but to survive. I am not teaching you; I am documenting my own rescue.
